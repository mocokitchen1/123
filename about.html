About St Mary's Clinic

Our Heritage

Located at 1 Hospital Road, Marianhill, Pinetown, St Mary's Clinic has served the South African community since 1995. We are dedicated to providing high-quality, professional healthcare with a personal touch.

Our Mission

To deliver compassionate healthcare through modern technology and skilled medical professionals.

Our Vision

To be the most efficient and patient-friendly community clinic in the region.

Why Our CRM Matters

By digitizing our records, we've eliminated the risk of lost files, reduced waiting times, and improved the accuracy of patient care. Every diagnosis and prescription is securely tracked to ensure your safety.
